# Napoleon at Austerlitz

Generate a portrait of Napoleon Bonaparte at the Battle of Austerlitz (1805),
the "Battle of the Three Emperors." This was Napoleon's greatest military
victory and the moment he considered the peak of his career.

## Visual Direction

- **Style**: Neoclassical oil painting in the tradition of Jacques-Louis David
- **Composition**: Three-quarter portrait, Napoleon on horseback or standing on
  the Pratzen Heights surveying the battlefield
- **Lighting**: Dawn breaking through morning fog — the famous "Sun of Austerlitz"
- **Uniform**: Grey overcoat over his green colonel's uniform of the Chasseurs
  à Cheval, bicorne hat worn sideways
- **Expression**: Calm, commanding confidence at the moment of victory
- **Details**: Battlefield smoke in the background, French tricolors visible,
  frozen December landscape of Moravia
