# Cleopatra Arriving in Rome

Generate a portrait of Cleopatra VII upon her arrival in Rome in 46 BC,
when she came as Julius Caesar's guest and caused a sensation in Roman society.
She would have been about 23 years old, a polyglot queen ruling the wealthiest
kingdom in the Mediterranean.
